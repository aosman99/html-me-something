Background

I was born in a country called Somalia. Although
my family was forced to flee due to war, I will
always call Somalia home.

My Journey

When I was just a few years of age, my family left Somalia.
We relocated to Kenya, where we stayed until I was seven years old.
Then my family moved to the United States where I have been living ever since.
I like to keep my roots so I often visit my family back home. These trips
are very humbling and I always return grateful that I am in a position
to help my family.

Currently

Fast forward to today. I am a student attending Seattle Central Community College.
I am also enrolled in a programming bootcamp with LaunchCode. My goal is to land
an internship with LaunchCode while continuing my studies and eventually get my degree
in computer science(God willing).
